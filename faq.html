Questions

Everything you need to know about Fractal Accelerator

◾ Program Details

What's the schedule like?

The accelerator is 60 hours per week for 12 weeks:

  • Monday-Friday: 9am - 7pm
  • Saturday: 10am - 4pm
  • Sunday: Off

This is a full-time, immersive program. You cannot work another job while enrolled.

What will I learn?

Languages: TypeScript and JavaScript (primary), with exposure to Python and Go

Tech Stack:

  • Frontend: React, Next.js, TailwindCSS
  • Backend: Node.js, Express
  • Database: Postgres, Redis
  • Infrastructure: AWS (Lambda, S3, RDS)

AI Modules: RAG systems, Model Context Protocol (MCP), streaming, and generative interfaces

How big is each cohort?

15 engineers maximum.

Small cohorts ensure you get direct access to instructors and build deep relationships with your peers. No fighting for attention in a class of 30+.

Can I really get a job without a CS degree?

Yes. 100% of our engineers who pursued SWE roles got hired.

You don't need a degree. You need real experience on real projects. That's what our internship program gives you—actual work you can point to in interviews.

What about AI replacing developers?

AI makes engineers MORE valuable, not less. The demand for engineers who can effectively use AI tools is skyrocketing.

You'll master AI tools from Day 1 (Cursor, Claude Code, v0, etc.) to work 10x faster than engineers who don't use them. This is your competitive advantage.

◾ Admissions Requirements

What prior experience do I need?

You should have basic familiarity with:

  • JavaScript or TypeScript basics
  • Git and command line
  • HTML/CSS fundamentals
  • React (at least basic understanding)

We'll send pre-course materials 4-6 weeks before the cohort starts to help you prepare.

Do I need a computer science degree?

No. A CS degree is not required. What matters is your ability to learn quickly, solve problems, and ship code.

Our engineers come from diverse backgrounds: teachers, PMs, consultants, sales professionals. They all got hired.

What equipment do I need?

macOS is required. We standardize on Mac to ensure environmental consistency across the cohort and match most startup development environments.
