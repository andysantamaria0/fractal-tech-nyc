METHODOLOGY & CIRR COMPLIANCE

Our placement statistics follow CIRR (Council on Integrity in Results Reporting) standards for transparency and accuracy.

How We Calculate Placement Rate:

  • Reporting period: 180 days after graduation
  • Only includes graduates actively seeking employment
  • Excludes students who opt out of job search or continue education
  • All salaries independently verified
